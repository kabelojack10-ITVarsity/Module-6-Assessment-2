About Us

At Auto for sale, the future begins today and we are constantly searching for new solutions that can usher in a new era of mobility, with innovations that are meaningful, sustainable and focused on people’s needs. With our range of innovative technologies and attractive models , Auto for sale is committed to pioneering the opportunities that digitalization and sustainable motoring can bring to our drivers and investors.

In South Africa, Auto for sale falls under the umbrella of Volkswagen Group South Africa (VWSA) which consists of the Audi, Volkswagen Passenger Cars and the Volkswagen Commercial Vehicles brands. Established in 1946, Volkswagen Group South Africa is a wholly owned subsidiary of Volkswagen Aktiengessellschaft (VWAG) in Germany. It is the largest German investment in South Africa and is a major contributor to foreign direct investment, technology transfer and skills development.

Auto for sale operates out of VWSA’s National Sales & Marketing organisation, which is located in Sandton, Johannesburg.
